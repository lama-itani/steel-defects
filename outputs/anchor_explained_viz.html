The Detection Process

1. Place Templates: 9 anchor boxes at every grid location (3 sizes × 3 shapes)

2. Pick & Adjust: Model selects anchors overlapping objects and refines them to fit exactly

3. Classify: Assigns object type and confidence score to each adjusted anchor


Why 9 anchors? Different sizes detect small to large objects. Different shapes (wide, square, tall) match object proportions.

Scale: For a 50×50 grid = 2,500 locations × 9 anchors = 22,500 templates scanning simultaneously.
